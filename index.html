Do I own the website?+
Yes. Once final payment is made, the website is yours — the files, the domain, everything. You can take it anywhere and do what you want with it. You are never renting it back from me.
Do I have to pay monthly?+
No. There are no required monthly fees. Maintenance at $100/month is completely optional — it's there if you want help with edits and updates. Skip it and the site still runs fine.
Can you use my Google reviews and photos?+
Yes, with your approval. I can pull your Google reviews and add them to the site. For photos, you send me what you have — job site shots, before-and-afters — and I'll format and place them properly.
How fast can you build it?+
Starter sites are typically built within 5–7 days once I have your content and photos. Full Site builds take a bit longer depending on scope. I'll give you a realistic timeline when we talk.
Do you only work with businesses in Florida?+
No — I work with local service businesses anywhere. I focus on businesses where customers search for a service in a city and call whoever looks most trustworthy. That's everywhere, not just Florida.
